# Code of Conduct

Stagecut contributors are expected to provide a welcoming, harassment-free environment. Be respectful, assume good intent, accept constructive feedback, and focus discussion on the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and publishing private information without permission. Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's contact channel. Maintainers will review reports promptly, preserve confidentiality where possible, and respond proportionately.
